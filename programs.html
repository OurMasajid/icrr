Programs

Faith, family, and learning at every stage of life. Browse what's running each week.

Daily

Five Daily Prayers

All five obligatory prayers in congregation, with short Khatira reminders after Fajr and Isha.

Weekly · Monday

Riyaad As-Saliheen

A weekly halaqah after Isha covering the noble hadith of the Prophet ﷺ from Imam An-Nawawi's Riyāḍ aṣ-Ṣāliḥīn.

Weekly · Sunday

Sunday School

Structured Islamic education for children — Qurʾān, ʿaqīdah, fiqh, and Islamic history. Ages K–12.

Weekly

Sisters' Halaqah

A nurturing weekly circle for sisters covering Qurʾān, hadith, and contemporary issues.

Weekly

Qurʾān Nāẓirah

Tajwīd-focused Qurʾān reading classes for new and improving readers, taught in small groups.

Tarbiya

Tarbiya Program

Character-building circles for youth that go beyond memorization — focusing on adab, ākhirah, and identity.

2nd Friday

Girls Night Out

A safe, fun monthly gathering for the masjid's young sisters — games, halaqahs, and snacks.

3rd Friday

Boys Night Out

Brothers' monthly youth night — sports, lecture, and pizza in the masjid's gym space.

1st Friday

Community Potluck

After Maghrib on the first Friday of each month — featuring a guest speaker and homemade dishes from across the community.

Summer

Summer Camp

A multi-week summer program for kids — Qurʾān, Arabic, sports, arts, and field trips.

Humanitarian

Free Food Pantry & Relief Assistance

ICRR partners with the Round Rock Free Food Pantry and the Greater Austin Muslim Relief Committee (GAMRC) to provide groceries and financial assistance to families in need — Muslim and non-Muslim alike.
