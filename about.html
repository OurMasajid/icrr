About the Islamic Center of Round Rock

A community-built masjid serving Round Rock, Texas since 2002 — through prayer, education, and humanitarian service.

Our Story

Twenty years of service, by the grace of Allah.

The Islamic Center of Round Rock (ICRR) was established in 2002 as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. What began as a small gathering of families has grown into a thriving community center that hosts daily prayers, weekend schools, family programs, and humanitarian outreach.

Today, ICRR serves Muslims across Round Rock, Pflugerville, Hutto, Cedar Park, and the surrounding North Austin area. We are committed to maintaining a welcoming space where every member — regardless of background or madhhab — feels at home.

Our Vision

A vibrant, God-conscious community that radiates the mercy and beauty of Islam to all who live in Round Rock.

Our Mission

  • Establish and maintain the daily prayers and weekly Jumuʿah in the best way.
  • Educate children and adults in Qurʾān, ʿaqīdah, fiqh, and the prophetic seerah.
  • Strengthen family ties through programs that engage youth, sisters, and elders.
  • Serve our broader community through humanitarian work and partnerships.
