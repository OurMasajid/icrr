Services

Care for the moments that matter — life, marriage, loss, and well-being.

Funeral (Janāzah) Service

The masjid provides Ghusl, Kafan, Janāzah prayer, and burial coordination according to the sunnah. Please submit a request form as early as possible so we can assist quickly.

  • Daytime contact: (512) 666-3389
  • Coordination with local cemeteries and funeral homes
  • Guidance for the family during this difficult time
Call (512) 666-3389 Request Janāzah form

Nikāḥ Service

Marriages are officiated by the Imam according to Islamic principles. We provide premarital counseling, the nikāḥ ceremony itself, and a signed nikāḥ contract.

  • Premarital meeting with the Imam
  • Two witnesses required (we can help arrange)
  • Documented mahr and contract
Request Nikāḥ

Free Medical Clinic

In partnership with Eixsys Healthcare, the masjid hosts a free walk-in clinic every Wednesday from 6:30–7:30 PM. Open to all — Muslims and non-Muslims.

  • Routine check-ups
  • Basic prescriptions
  • Health screenings

Counseling & Family Support

Confidential meetings with the Imam for spiritual, marital, and family concerns. Please call ahead to set up a time.
