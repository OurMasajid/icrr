About ICRR

A community rooted in faith, learning, and service.

Established in 2002 as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, the Islamic Center of Round Rock is a place of worship, education, and connection. We open our doors to every Muslim and welcome our neighbors with open hearts.

Learn more about us

Our Vision

A vibrant, God-conscious community that radiates the mercy of Islam to all who live in Round Rock.

Our Mission

To serve God through prayer, knowledge, family-centered programs, and humanitarian work.

Imam & Shura

Spiritual leadership and an elected Shura that guides the masjid's affairs with transparency.

Volunteer

Join the hands that keep this masjid running — every act of service is a sadaqah jariyah.

Programs

Programs for every member of the family.

From the first prayer of the day to youth gatherings, education, and humanitarian work — there's a place for you here.

Daily Programs

Five daily prayers in congregation, with Khatira reminders after Fajr and Isha.

Educational Programs

Quran Nazira, Sisters' Halaqah, Sunday School, Tarbiya, and Summer Camp.

Youth Activities

Girls Night Out (2nd Friday) & Boys Night Out (3rd Friday) every month.

Humanitarian Assistance

Free Food Pantry partnership and financial assistance via the Greater Austin Muslim Relief Committee.

Monthly Programs

Community potluck with a guest speaker on the 1st Friday of every month.

Spiritual Enrichment

Riyaad As-Saliheen Monday sessions on the noble hadith of the Prophet ﷺ.
